Curated Reads

A selection of books that shaped my perspective, sparked creativity, or taught something unforgettable.

  • Shrimad Bhagwad Geeta – Timeless wisdom for life and consciousness.
  • The Ultimate Gift – Lessons on purpose, gratitude, and personal growth.
  • The Alchemist – A story about following your dreams and listening to the universe.
  • The Immortals of Meluha – Mythical storytelling with rich historical imagination.
  • The Secret of the Nagas – Adventure and mystery intertwined with Indian mythology.
  • The Oath of the Vayuputras – Epic conclusion to a gripping mythological saga.
  • A Little Life – Deeply moving narrative on trauma, friendship, and resilience.
  • A Good Girl’s Guide to Murder – Fast-paced, clever mystery with twists.
  • Good Girl, Bad Blood – The thrilling continuation of a compelling detective story.
  • As Good As Dead – Edge-of-your-seat suspense and investigation.
  • Mark of the Thief – Adventure in a vividly imagined Roman world.
  • Eleven Minutes – Exploring love, relationships, and self-discovery.
  • The Forty Rules of Love – Spirituality, Sufism, and transformative love.
  • The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari – Practical life lessons on simplicity, happiness, and mindfulness.
  • Fairy Tales – Magical stories that shaped imagination and creativity.
  • The Hidden Hindu – A deep dive into Indian philosophy and spirituality.
